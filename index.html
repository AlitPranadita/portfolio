About Me.

I am a graduate of Informatics Engineering from the Indonesia Institute of Business and Technology (INSTIKI) with a strong interest in technology and system development. During my studies, I worked on various projects including web programming, web programming frameworks, mobile programming, web design, computer networks, and databases, which equipped me with solid technical understanding and strong problem-solving skills. I was also actively involved in the Student Association and participated as a committee member in various events, allowing me to develop teamwork, effective communication, and strong responsibility management. I am a disciplined, fast learner who can easily adapt to new environments. I am eager to continue developing my skills and contribute positively in a professional work environment.
